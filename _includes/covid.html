Covid-19 FAQ

Due to the Covid-19 pandemic, BSides decided to initially postpone until November, but as uncertainty about large gatherings and international travel was growing, it was decided to postpone it to 2021. The new date is now set to {{site.date}} and will still be at {{ site.location }}.

This does decision does not come lightly, but the safety and well-being of participants, speakers and volunteers is our number one priority. We're very grateful for your continued support of BSides Oslo, and we'll make sure that it's well worth the wait. Some of you might have some questions, so we'll try to answer your questions in this section.

Has the conference been moved?
Nope, it'll be at {{ site.location}} in 2021 as well.

I bought a ticket, is it still valid?
Sure is, all tickets bought for the original date is valid for {{ site.date }}.

I haven't bought a ticket yet, will you open up ticket sales again?
It will re-open at a later date, so keep an eye on Twitter for announcements about ticket sales.

I've had time to think of an awesome subject, can I still submit a presentation?
The CFP will be re-launched as the new date approaches. Make sure to follow us on Twitter for updates on when it's up again.

The new date doesn't work for me. What's your return policy?
Refund until 4 weeks before the event, minus the Deltager.no fees. Send us an e-mail for more info.

If your questions wasn't answered, feel free to e-mail us.
