Covid-19 Information

Due to growing concerns surrounding the outbreak of the Covid-19 virus, we've decided to postpone BSides 2020 until November.

This decision does not come lightly and we've been monitoring the situation closely for the last few week. Even though our event is small the health, safety and well-being of our patrons is paramount. We will continue working with our sponsors, partners and the community to make sure the conference will be worth the wait.

We won't be cancelling tickets, and we thank you for your continued support. Information about our refund policy is available here. If you have any questions surrounding this decision, just e-mail us.
